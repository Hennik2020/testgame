Crystal Raiders

Survive waves of crystalline foes, upgrade your gear, and chase the high score.

How to Play

  • Move with W A S D
  • Aim with the mouse, shoot with Left Click or hold Space
  • Open the shop between waves with B or the Shop button
  • Buy upgrades using credits dropped by enemies
  • Survive as many waves as you can!
